Project I.C.E. (Ice Core Excavator)

This project was part of research at Colorado Space Grant Consortium and aimed to create an attachment to allow a drone to drill and collect an ice core sample. The project was successful and delivered in September 2023.

Drill Powered Bike

This project was part of the Component Design class at CU Boulder in which students would fully design and build a bike that could be powered by a cordless drill. Our team's bike was wildly successful and went on to be the fastest bike out of the 29 groups (1st place in drag race).

Electric Powered Wheelchair

This project was part of a freshman projects class in which our team attempted to design a low-cost device that could turn any manual wheelchair into a powered one. In the end, the project kind of worked, but only on flat surfaces as the motors would slip otherwise.
